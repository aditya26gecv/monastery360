About Sikkim

Sikkim, nestled in the lap of the Himalayas, is home to over 200 monasteries. The state is famous for its serene landscapes, Buddhist culture, and rich spiritual heritage that blends seamlessly with nature.

Our Mission

Monastery 360 is dedicated to digitally preserving and showcasing the monasteries of Sikkim. Through immersive experiences, we connect travelers and researchers with the cultural treasures of the Himalayas.
